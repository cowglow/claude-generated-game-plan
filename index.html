Progression · double progression

Start at a weight you can do for 8 reps with 1–2 left in the tank. Add reps each session. Hit 12 on all sets → add weight, drop back to 8. Track only these four. Numbers slowly climbing = you're winning.

The 4 rules

1
Protein at every meal
A palm-sized portion at breakfast, lunch, dinner. Keeps you full, protects muscle while fat drops. If you get one rule right, it's this.
2
Half the plate is veg or salad
Lunch and dinner. Doesn't matter which. Volume without much energy = full without overeating.
3
Don't drink your calories
Water, coffee, tea, zero-sugar — yes. Juice, soda, sugary coffees, beer most nights — no. Removes huge energy with zero food willpower.
4
One fist of starch per meal
Carbs are fine — just right-sized. One fist, not the whole plate. You're not cutting, you're portioning.

Default meals — repeat these when you can't think

▸ Eggs + leftover veg + toast ▸ Chicken/quark + rice + frozen veg ▸ Tinned fish + salad bag + bread ▸ Quark/yogurt + berries + nuts

Boring and repeatable beats varied and unsustainable. Rotate forever.

Fast food — realistic, not "never"

  • Cap at 2/week, planned on specific days — a decision, not a default
  • Grilled over fried · single over double
  • Skip the sugary drink — halves the damage alone
  • Burger + water beats any "meal deal"

Fallback for stressful days

The goal isn't "eat well" — it's don't blow it up. Minimum standard:

  • Get protein in — a shake or rotisserie chicken counts
  • Don't drink calories
  • A "boring protein + water" day is a success, not a failure

Emergency plan

You fell off for 3–7 days

This is the section that decides whether a bad week becomes a bad year. Last time, the rebound came from a slip turning into a long break. This page exists to end it in one day.

1
No accounting, no guilt, no "starting Monday." The cost of a bad week is the bad week — not a punishment week after it.
2
Next meal, not next week. Recovery starts at your very next meal: protein + water. That's the whole reset.
3
Easy gym session within 48 hours. Not to make up for it — just to break the seal. 30 minutes is plenty.
4
Re-anchor with one default meal today. Restock the always-in-the-house ingredients if needed.
5
"Never two in a row" starts now. The break is over the instant you decide it is.
